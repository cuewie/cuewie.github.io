Work & Contributions

Tap any card to learn more

I helped with the second Thailand creator meetup (in-person) in Bangkok in October 2025. As a volunteer ambassador for Roblox in Thailand, I support the growth of the local developer and player community. I do this by building partnerships, organizing events, and contributing to community-driven initiatives. My role involves empowering creators and strengthening the Thai Roblox ecosystem, while also maintaining updates on community activities, news, and public engagement.

RTCN is an organization dedicated to bringing Thai Roblox players together, supporting various initiatives, and organizing events to strengthen community connections. We are passionate about the Roblox platform and the Metaverse. The organization focuses on developing and promoting communication within the Roblox Thailand community.

I helped manage the Facebook page as well as support inquiries from messages received on the page. I also helped with some graphic and UI work for promotional content.

I am a community moderator for Walmart Discovered, which is Walmart's Roblox game. I ensure member safety and civility, as well as communication amongst the team.

Researched and fact-checked information related to the Roblox community and all things Roblox.

Customer service, backend tracking, paperwork, and general post office operations.

I'm part of Roblox's beta program and also this program, which is a localization program created by Roblox's Developer Relations team. In this program, we suggest features, offer feedback on Roblox's translation tools, features, and products, and share opinions and insightful knowledge with the Roblox localization team.

I translated the Roblox horror game Specter (also known as Specter 2 on launch). I've translated the game from English to Thai, localizing the game for the Thai Roblox community. This was a fun and challenging project.

I was a volunteer QA at Dubit, testing various Roblox games and projects that the company created or collaborated on with another entity. I provided feedback, insights, and suggestions to the company and its partners.

I joined the first-ever MGL event back in 2021 through Roblox advertisements. I was heavily involved and then proceeded to apply and become an advisor and server moderator for MGL. I answer questions, escalate issues, communicate closely with the team, and ensure the server is fun and safe for all users.

I've translated the Roblox game from English to Thai, providing localization to the Thai community of Free Draw. My translations can even be spotted on a Thai creator's YouTube video!
